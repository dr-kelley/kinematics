Learning Objectives

Conceptual Understanding

  • • Distinguish between transverse and longitudinal waves
  • • Understand the principle of superposition and wave interference
  • • Explain how standing waves form from wave reflection
  • • Describe the Doppler effect qualitatively and quantitatively

Mathematical Skills

  • • Use the wave equation to relate speed, frequency, and wavelength
  • • Identify harmonic frequencies for strings and open/closed tubes
  • • Determine conditions for constructive and destructive interference
  • • Apply the Doppler formula to calculate observed frequencies
