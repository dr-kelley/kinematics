Learning Objectives

Conceptual Understanding

  • • Recognize the rotational analogs of displacement, velocity, and acceleration
  • • Understand how torque produces angular acceleration
  • • Explain why mass distribution affects moment of inertia
  • • Understand conservation of angular momentum and its consequences

Mathematical Skills

  • • Calculate torque from force magnitude, lever arm, and angle
  • • Apply τ = Iα to find angular acceleration of rigid bodies
  • • Use conservation of angular momentum in collision problems
  • • Include rotational kinetic energy in energy conservation equations
