Learning Objectives

Conceptual Understanding

  • • Distinguish between kinetic, potential, and thermal energy
  • • Understand conservation of mechanical energy in isolated systems
  • • Identify conservative and non-conservative forces
  • • Interpret potential energy diagrams to predict system behavior

Mathematical Skills

  • • Calculate work done by constant and variable forces
  • • Apply the work-energy theorem to find speeds and displacements
  • • Use conservation of energy to solve problems without knowing forces
  • • Calculate power and efficiency in mechanical systems
