Learning Objectives

Conceptual Understanding

  • • Understand Newton's three laws of motion and their applications
  • • Recognize action-reaction force pairs in physical systems
  • • Distinguish between static and kinetic friction
  • • Understand elastic forces and energy storage in springs

Mathematical Skills

  • • Draw and analyze free body diagrams
  • • Apply Newton's second law to calculate acceleration from forces
  • • Calculate friction forces using coefficients and normal forces
  • • Apply Hooke's Law to spring systems
