Learning Objectives

Conceptual Understanding

  • • Understand the conditions that produce simple harmonic motion
  • • Recognize the relationship between SHM and circular motion
  • • Understand how wave motion arises from coupled oscillators
  • • Describe how damping and driving forces affect oscillatory systems

Mathematical Skills

  • • Use sinusoidal functions to describe displacement, velocity, and acceleration
  • • Calculate period and frequency for spring-mass and pendulum systems
  • • Relate wave speed, wavelength, and frequency
  • • Analyze energy transformations between kinetic and potential forms
